What is it?

What? Where? When? is a realtime, multiplayer, competitive game. Challenge your friends to find out who is the smartest one.

How to play?

All you have to do is answer questions. You gain points for correct answers. At the end scoreboard will report the best player of the match. Click on a room below or create your own.
